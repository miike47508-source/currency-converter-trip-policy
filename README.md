Currency Converter Trip ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use the App, why it is processed, and your choices.

## 1. Information We Process

### A) Camera (android.permission.CAMERA)
- **Purpose:** To enable camera-based price/text scanning features.
- **How it is used:** Camera access is used to capture input for on-device processing in app features.
- **Important:** We do not upload your camera images/videos to our own servers by default.

### B) Location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)
- **Purpose:** To estimate your local/home currency based on your current country/location.
- **How it is used:** The App checks location permission, gets device location, and uses geocoding to infer country/currency.
- **Important:** We do not sell your location data.

### C) Overlay and Lock-Screen Related Permissions
- **Permissions/Capabilities:** `SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW`, foreground service, boot completed receiver.
- **Purpose:** Optional lock-screen quick launch/auto-launch features on Android.
- **How it is used:** Only if you enable related settings in the App.

### D) Network Access (INTERNET, ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
- **Purpose:** To fetch exchange rates and support ad delivery.
- **How it is used:** The App requests exchange-rate data and ad resources over the network.

### E) Local App Storage
The App stores certain settings and usage state locally on your device (for example via SharedPreferences), such as:
- selected currencies and app preferences,
- calculator/feature settings,
- lock-screen feature settings,
- local app state needed for functionality.

## 2. Third-Party Services

These third parties may process data under their own privacy policies.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

## 4. Data Retention
